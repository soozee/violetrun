4/20/02
So yeah, we're playing this casino in Elko for a Battle of the Bands competition. We don't know whether or not we're going to loved or hated by the country masses (all there to see Nevada-based country cover band Mud Puppy, of course), but we'll see. Dodging tomatoes along with us will be our friends from Provo, Gift Anon, melodic velvet-emo masters who will be providing the van to get to Elko in. Thanks, Gift Anon.

Also under breaking news is: Randal lost his job a couple weeks ago and Sean lost his on Friday. Cute, huh? Randal quit and was then told to not come back (does that qualify as being fired?) and the business Sean was in went under. Sean's getting married in May, though, so that should help alleviate at least some of the pain.

Our CD's coming out on May 9th at the Urban Lounge. The title's East of the Sun, West of the Moon and the theme of our CD sleeve is "dark fairy tales." The title is actually the title of a book of fairy tales from the 1930s. We're not saying anything beyond that, because it's s'posed to be a surprise, but do know that the first people who buy a CD at the CD release party will also get a homemade velvet baggie for it to go in (until we run out, that is). Also, there may be cupcakes available, seeing as how the next day is Rebecca's birthday (May 10).

We're excited about playing the Dark Arts Fest in June along with about a dozen other local bands and several national headliners. For more info, please see www.slcsanctuary.com

And, we're trying to set up a show in May with local politi-Goth wunder-band Tragic Black. Stay tuned.
